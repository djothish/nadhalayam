Welcome to Nadhalayam

A sanctuary where Kerala's rich artistic heritage comes alive. Since 2012, we've nurtured hundreds of students in Carnatic music, Bharatanatyam, Mohiniyattam, and classical instrumental arts — guided by tradition, shaped with love.

ഞങ്ങളെക്കുറിച്ച്

A Decade of Dedication

Founded in 2012 in the quiet coastal town of Panavally, Nadhalayam began as a humble dream — to preserve and pass on the classical arts of Kerala. Over the years, under the guidance of Sri Thripunthura Radhakrishnan Sir and a team of dedicated gurus, it has grown into a cherished institution.

Today, with branches in Panavally and Aroor, we serve students across Kerala, India, and abroad — offering both in-person and online training.
